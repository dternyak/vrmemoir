Frequently Asked Questions

How long will my recording be?
Our standard package includes up to an hour of immersive 3D recording, using our state of the art 8K 3D 360 VR Camera.
How will the recording be created?
You'll direct our professional team to a location of your choosing. Our clients typically use a familiar environment like their home for the setting of the recording. We'll setup our recording system in your home, and begin recording!
What exactly am I buying?
You're buying a state of the art method of preserving the legacy of yourself or a loved one. Our professional team will record family stories and life lessons with our tried-and-tested interview script in next-generation Virtual Reality. We'll provide you with a copy of your recording in digital format, and teach you and your family members how to access your new recording.
What should I say in the recording?
We provide a tried-and-tested interview script to help guide the conversation to the stories that your family cares about most. In addition to using our script, you're welcome to tell specific stories that you want to preserve for the generations to come.
How much will it cost?
Our standard cost for a 1-hour session is $495. We're happy to work out a custom arrangement should you wish to record a series of sessions at a reduced price.
When will I receive the recording?
Once we finish the recording, our team will review and process the raw recording. You'll receive a link to your email with instructions on how to access your recording within 1 week of our recording session.
